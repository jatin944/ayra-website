Why Ayra exists

Brands face an audit they cannot pass. Recyclers run on margins they cannot see. Workers do work that no one counts.

01 / Brands

From self-attested to verifiable.

Tamper-evident chain-of-custody and DigiLocker-issued EPR certificates replace paperwork with proof — audit-ready for BRSR, CSRD, CBAM.

02 / Operators

One system, every kilogram.

Pickups, mass-balance sorting, bale management, inventory, sales — replacing WhatsApp logs with realtime operational and financial KPIs.

03 / Recyclers

A price signal that finally exists.

ONDC integration breaks local-monopoly pricing. Verified low-carbon feedstock unlocks 5–15% premium realisation from brands chasing recycled-content targets.

04 / Workers

Identity. Dignity. Trail.

Aadhaar-based onboarding brings informal kabadiwallahs into formal systems with payment trails — financial inclusion as a side-effect of recycling.
